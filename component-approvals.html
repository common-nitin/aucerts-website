Approval support for vehicle components

Vehicle components and systems often need separate approval before they can be used in vehicle production, modification or market entry. Aucerts supports component manufacturers, suppliers and importers with component approval planning, documentation and testing coordination.

We can assist with Australian, ECE, EU, Indian and other international approval support depending on the component type and target market.

E-mark and ECE component approval support

For components intended for markets using UNECE regulations, we can support E-mark and ECE approval activities through suitable partner networks and approval routes. This may include support for E4, E5, E9 and E11-related approval strategies where applicable.

Our role can include technical documentation preparation, test planning, communication support, review of information documents, COP assistance and certificate maintenance.
