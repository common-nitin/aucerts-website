Vehicle approval support for Australia

Supplying a vehicle to the Australian market requires the correct approval pathway and properly prepared compliance information. For manufacturers and importers, this process can be time-consuming because each applicable ADR must be reviewed and supported with the correct technical evidence.

Aucerts assists clients in preparing vehicle type approval applications and other vehicle approval submissions. We can support passenger vehicles, buses, commercial vehicles, trailers and special-purpose vehicle projects.

Applications can require

  • Vehicle make, model and variant information
  • Applicable ADR list and evidence matrix
  • Test reports, certificates and drawings
  • Component compliance evidence
  • Manufacturer and production details
  • RAV entry and submission information
