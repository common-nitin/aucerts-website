Aucerts Pty Ltd is an Australian engineering and compliance support business focused on vehicle approvals, automotive homologation, technical documentation and product engineering services.

About Aucerts Pty Ltd

Aucerts supports manufacturers, importers, component suppliers, body builders and engineering organisations that need assistance with regulatory approval, compliance evidence, testing coordination and design verification.

Our work covers Australian Design Rules, ROVER submissions, vehicle type approvals, component approvals, ECE and EU homologation support, Indian homologation support and international standards assistance through established professional partnerships.

Our strength is the combination of engineering understanding and regulatory experience. We do not look at compliance as only an administrative task. We look at the full product: design, application, installation, technical evidence, testing requirement, manufacturing control and approval pathway.

Our mission

Our mission is to make vehicle compliance and engineering approval work clearer, better organised and more practical for manufacturers, importers and technical businesses.

We aim to support clients with reliable advice, structured documentation and professional coordination so they can move forward with confidence.

What makes us different

Aucerts provides a practical bridge between product engineering and approval requirements. We can review technical drawings, understand vehicle structure, assess component applications, prepare compliance reports, coordinate testing and support approval submissions.
