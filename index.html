What we do

Vehicle compliance, engineering and homologation support

Bringing a vehicle, trailer, component or engineered product into a regulated market requires the right standards, correct test evidence, structured documentation and a practical approval strategy.

01

Vehicle Type Approval

Support for ADR review, ROVER documentation, vehicle approval applications and concessional RAV entry preparation.
02

Component Approval

Technical documentation, test planning and approval support for regulated vehicle components and systems.
03

Engineering / CAD / FEA

Mechanical design, CAD modelling, finite element analysis and engineering justification support.
04

Testing Arrangements

Coordination of vehicle and component testing through suitable laboratories and technical partners.
05

International Homologation

Support for ECE, EU, E-mark, Indian, GSO and other international approval requirements through partner networks.
06

Regulation Support

Standard updates, interpretation, technical clause review, translation support and compliance gap assessment.
